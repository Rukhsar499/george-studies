Welcome To George School Studies

Established in 1920, The George Telegraph Group, is going to add another wing, ‘George School Studies’ with its body for the enhancement of the academic excellence of the students. George School Studies, an initiative of The George Telegraph Group, is ardently dedicated to the enhancement of knowledge and strengthening the future of the generation by involving them in a process of gradual transformation of aspects of knowledge.

With the combination of traditional values and modern teaching techniques, this initiative ensures quality education by creating a team of skilled and dedicated teaching professionals. To upgrade the academic performance of the students, George School Studies is intended to focus on a student- centric learning environment and intensify the critical thinking and time management ability.
