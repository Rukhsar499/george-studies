Who We Are

Recognized as an esteemed educational institute, the eminence of The George Telegraph Group received immense appreciation from numerous commercial houses along with Government departments and Chambers of Commerce. Considering the requirement of the contemporary industry, The George Telegraph Group introduced educational and vocational program which brought this educational institute at the limelight of the discussion. With the aim to prepare students of average merit for the world of employment, this institute marked an impression by continuously introducing new vocational training courses, concentrating on the individual potential of the student. The standardized methodology technique of the institute accompanied by a highly qualified team of teaching professionals relentlessly contributed to the personality development of the students which made them confident in the employment sector. Established in 1920, this organization is now adding another wing, ‘George School Studies’ with its body for the enhancement of the academic excellence of the students. George School Studies, an initiative of The George Telegraph Group, is ardently dedicated to the enhancement of knowledge and strengthening the future of the generation by involving them in a process of gradual transformation of aspects of knowledge. With the combination of traditional values and modern teaching techniques, this initiative ensures quality education by creating a team of skilled and dedicated teaching professionals. To upgrade the academic performance of the students, George School Studies is intended to focus on a student- centric learning environment and intensify the critical thinking and time management ability

The primary objective of this initiative is to offer interactive and engaging offline learning classes for students ranging from class IX to class XII along with regular assessment and mock test series. Based on the latest exam pattern in West Bengal Board, this initiative has also introduced well-research study materials with interactive doubt-clearing classes by the association of experienced subject experts. In addition, this institution also provides classroom facilities for engineering and medical entrance examinations for all board students along with regular tests and assessment sessions. With the assistance of experienced faculty members, this unit also provides well-analyzed and comprehensive study materials with doubt-clearing sessions in a competitive study environment covering all the courses at the said time

Our Mission

With the objective to brighten the professional development of the future generation, this educational institute aims at providing inductive teaching approach while concentrating on individual potential of the students by encouraging them to exercise analytical thinking and inculcate values.
